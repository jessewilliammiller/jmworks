Jesse Miller

Jesse Miller creates music and plays basketball in Seattle, Washington. He has been involved with the production of over thirty releases of his own music and others' since 2010. Jesse's last album, Our Fodder, took a departure from his usual guitar and vocal driven songs to explore the world of instrumental dance/beat music. Currently he has stepped back into playing guitar, singing, and producing experimental pop songs with a rotating cast of talented friends. A new collection of pop songs is emerging from Jesse's studio and public performances that he hopes to finish up soon. You can also catch Jesse as a sideman in Jonny G and the Music Factory, Rachel Ewen, and Peg.
