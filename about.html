Jesse Miller
Works | Shows | Bio | Sync | Vids | Merch

Jesse Miller creates music and plays basketball in Seattle, Washington. He has been involved with over thirty releases since 2010. Currently a sideman in Jonny G & the Music Factory, Joe Waine, Rachel Ewen, and Peg, he also plays original music as J.M. Fodder. His last album, Our Fodder, took a departure from his usual guitar driven songs to explore the world of avant-garde electronic dance music. Currently Jesse is producing new original pop songs, and working on expanding the J.M. Fodder live band.
